You wear your heart on your sleeve. You are extremely hard-working yet soft-spoken and subversively powerful. All of these qualities represent that of the great RBG. RBG was was an American lawyer and jurist who served as an associate justice of the Supreme Court of the United States from 1993 to 2020.

Which Feminist Are You?

Alexandria Ocasio Cortez

You have a fiery personality and will always stand up for what is right. You are unapologetically yourself and strive to make a real difference in the world. You are down to earth, practical, and passionate. All of these qualities represent that of Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez aka AOC. AOC is an American politician serving as the U.S. Representative for New York's 14th congressional district since 2019 - she is the youngest woman to ever serve Congress. AOC gave one of the best feminist speeches of all time after Ted Yoho called her a “f*cking b*tch” right on the House floor.

Which Feminist Are You?

Simone de Beauvoir

You are optimistic, methodical, and intellectual. You use your creativity in everything that you do. All of these qualities represent that of Simone de Beauvoir. Simone de Beauvoir was a French writer, activist, and feminist icon. She Is the author of one of the most renowned feminist books of all time, “The Second Sex.” She had a significant influence on both feminist existentialism and feminist theory. 

Which Feminist Are You?

Malala Yousafzai

You are a fighter. No matter the consequences, you will always fight for what you believe in. You are persistent, charismatic, and always full of life. All of these qualities represent that of Malala Yousafzai. Malala is a Pakistani activist for female education and the youngest Nobel Prize laureate. As a 16 year old girl, she defied the Taliban in Pakistan, demanding a girl’s right to an education. She was shot in the head three times during a campaign but survived. She has helped over 130 million girls get an education.
