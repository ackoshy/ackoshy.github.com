Game of Pig

Earn money for your piggy bank!

• Objective: Be the first to earn $30 in your piggy bank

• There are 2 players. Each player takes turn rolling die

• The total of the roll is added to the player’s balance unless one of the die lands on 1

• If either die lands on “one”, the current player’s turn ends and it is the other player’s turn

• If you roll two ones, your current balance is reset to $0

• After each roll the current player can roll again or pass (assuming a 1 was not rolled)

• The first player to $30 wins!
